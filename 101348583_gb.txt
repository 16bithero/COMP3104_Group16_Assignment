George Brown College has multiple locations in Toronto which are in Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront

Casa Loma - 160 Kendal Ave, Toronto, ON M5R 1M3
St. James - 200 King St E, Toronto, ON M5A 3W8
Waterfront - 51 Dockside Dr, Toronto, ON M5A 0B6

George Brown College offers diploma programs, advanced diploma programs, and two conjunction programs
associated with Toronto Metropolitan University

The building A in Casa Loma was torned down in 2004 due to students experiencing
illness.
